    Disney Movies 1937-2016 Gross Income

    This dataset is from Kaggle and it contains information on the total gross earnings of various Disney movies from the CSV file sample. The dataset includes 5 columns: Movie Title (the name of the Disney movie), Release Date (the year the movie was released), Genre (the movie genre or type), MPAA Rating (the audience rating classification), and Total Gross (the total box office gross earnings in dollars).
