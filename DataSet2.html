Disney Characters Success

This dataset is from Kaggle and it contains information on the success of various Disney characters based on their appearances, merchandise, and popularity. The dataset includes 5 columns: Character Name (the name of the Disney character), Number of Appearances (how many movies or shows the character appeared in), Merchandise Sales (total merchandise revenue generated), Popularity Score (a rating of the character's popularity), and Franchise Value (the overall value the character brings to the Disney franchise).
